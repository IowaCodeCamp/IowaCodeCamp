Notes for the Day

Some things to note for the day of the code camp:

  1. The closest parking area is across the street in the paid parking ramp. The first hour is 75 cents and each additional hour is 90 cents.
  2. Link to IMU directions page (http://imu.uiowa.edu/maps-directions/)
  3. Drinks will be provided during breakfast and lunch, but will be limited to water during the morning with one pop break during the afternoon, which will consist of Coke products. If you want other drinks, please bring them with you or bring money for the available vending machines.
